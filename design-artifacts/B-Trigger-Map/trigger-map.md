# Trigger Map - trustme-shop

**Phase 2: Strategic Psychology Mapping**
**Date:** 2026-03-17

---

## 1. Business Goals & Objectives

**Goal 1: Complete Shopping & Management Flow**
- Objective 1.1: Users can seamlessly browse, add to cart, and checkout.
- Objective 1.2: Admins can efficiently process orders and manage the catalog.

**Goal 2: Innovative Shopping Experience**
- Objective 2.1: Successfully integrate AI Chat Widget.
- Objective 2.2: Increase user engagement through personalized suggestions.

---

## 2. Target Groups (Prioritized)

### Priority 1: Khách hàng mua lẻ (Retail Customers)
**Who They Are:** 
Individuals shopping for clothes for work and leisure. They value convenience and look for guided shopping experiences.

**Psychological Profile:**
They think in terms of "outfits" (mix & match) rather than individual items. They are influenced by the current weather and context (e.g., work vs. going out).

**Usage Context:**
They visit the site seeking inspiration or needing an outfit quickly. They will heavily rely on the AI Chat Widget if they feel stuck.

**Relationship to Goals:** 
Directly drives Goal 1 (Shopping Flow) and Goal 2 (Engagement via AI).

### Priority 2: Quản trị viên (Shop Owners/Staff)
**Who They Are:**
The team managing the operational side of Trustme-shop.

**Psychological Profile:**
They value speed, efficiency, and clarity. They want a streamlined interface to get things done without hassle.

**Usage Context:**
Daily operations: posting new items, updating stock, managing orders, and pulling real-time revenue reports.

**Relationship to Goals:**
Directly drives Goal 1 (Management Flow).

---

## 3. Driving Forces

### Priority 1: Khách hàng mua lẻ
**Negative Drivers (Fears/Frustrations):**
1. **Fear of bad combinations:** Anxious about buying individual items that don't mix and match well together. (High Intensity, High Frequency)
2. **Search Fatigue:** Frustrated by wasting time scrolling through hundreds of items to find a complete outfit. (High Intensity, High Frequency)

**Positive Drivers (Wishes/Desires):**
3. **Desire for Guided Outfits:** Wants a complete outfit suggestion tailored to the current weather and context without thinking too hard. (High Intensity, Mod Frequency)

### Priority 2: Quản trị viên
**Negative Drivers (Fears/Frustrations):**
1. **Inefficiency:** Dislikes repetitive, slow tasks when posting products or processing orders. (High Intensity, High Frequency)
2. **Lack of Insight:** Frustrated by not having quick access to real-time sales numbers to make decisions. (High Intensity, Mod Frequency)

**Positive Drivers (Wishes/Desires):**
3. **Operational Speed:** Wants fast posting functionalities and convenient, clear dashboards.
4. **Real-time Reporting:** Desires instant revenue and statistical reports to track shop performance.

---

## 4. Strategic Focus Statement

> **Design Focus:** We will prioritize the **Khách hàng mua lẻ**, specifically designing the AI Chat Widget to solve their **fear of bad outfit combinations** and **search fatigue** by proactively offering complete, context-aware outfit suggestions. For the **Quản trị viên**, the admin dashboard will focus on **operational speed** with fast data entry and real-time revenue reporting.
