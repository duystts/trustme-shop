# Persona: Khách hàng mua lẻ (Retail Customer)

**Who They Are:** 
Individuals aged 18-35 who frequently shop online for clothes suitable for work and leisure. They value convenience, trends, and look for guided shopping experiences because they don't have the time or fashion expertise to put together outfits from scratch.

**Psychological Profile:**
They think in terms of "outfits" (mix & match) rather than individual items. They are highly influenced by the current weather, the specific occasion (e.g., an office meeting vs. a weekend cafe hangout), and trending styles. They want to feel confident in what they wear without the mental load of styling it themselves.

**Internal State:**
When browsing online, they start out hopeful but quickly become **overwhelmed and fatigued** if presented with too many options. They experience **anxiety** about whether a top will match the bottoms they own or if the complete set will look cohesive. When they see a well-put-together outfit suggestion, they feel **relieved and inspired**.

**Usage Context:**
They visit the site seeking inspiration or needing an outfit quickly for an upcoming event. They typically scan the homepage for new arrivals or curated collections. If they can't find what they need within a few minutes, they will drop off. They will heavily rely on the AI Chat Widget if they feel stuck or want a quick recommendation based on today's weather.

**Relationship to Business Goals:** 
- ✅ **Complete Shopping Flow:** They are the primary source of revenue; a smooth checkout process ensures conversion.
- ✅ **Innovative Shopping Experience:** Their usage of the AI Chat Widget for outfit recommendations is the core differentiator of the platform.

**Key Driving Forces:**
- 🔴 **Fear of bad combinations:** Anxious about buying individual items that don't mix and match well together. (High Intensity, High Frequency)
- 🔴 **Search Fatigue:** Frustrated by wasting time scrolling through hundreds of items to find a complete outfit. (High Intensity, High Frequency)
- 🟢 **Desire for Guided Outfits:** Wants a complete outfit suggestion tailored to the current weather and context without thinking too hard. (High Intensity, Mod Frequency)
