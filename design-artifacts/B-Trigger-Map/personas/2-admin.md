# Persona: Quản trị viên (Shop Owner/Staff)

**Who They Are:**
The team managing the operational side of Trustme-shop. This includes shop owners who need high-level overviews and staff members who handle day-to-day tasks like order fulfillment and inventory updates.

**Psychological Profile:**
They value speed, efficiency, and clarity above all else in their tools. They treat the management dashboard as a workspace, not an entertainment platform. They want a streamlined interface to get things done without hassle, minimizing clicks and cognitive load. They are protective of their time.

**Internal State:**
When logging into the admin dashboard, they are in a **focused and task-oriented** mindset. They feel **frustrated and impatient** if the system is slow, requires repetitive data entry, or if finding essential information is difficult. When they can instantly see sales trends or process an order with one click, they feel **in control and productive**.

**Usage Context:**
Daily operations: posting new items, updating stock, managing orders, and pulling real-time revenue reports. They access the dashboard frequently throughout the day, often needing to perform quick actions between other tasks. A clear, information-dense (but not cluttered) UI is essential for their workflow.

**Relationship to Business Goals:**
- ✅ **Complete Management Flow:** Their ability to efficiently manage the catalog and process orders ensures the operational success of the e-commerce platform, directly supporting the back-end objectives of the project.

**Key Driving Forces:**
- 🔴 **Inefficiency:** Dislikes repetitive, slow tasks when posting products or processing orders. (High Intensity, High Frequency)
- 🔴 **Lack of Insight:** Frustrated by not having quick access to real-time sales numbers to make decisions. (High Intensity, Mod Frequency)
- 🟢 **Operational Speed:** Wants fast posting functionalities and convenient, clear dashboards. (High Intensity, High Frequency)
- 🟢 **Real-time Reporting:** Desires instant revenue and statistical reports to track shop performance. (High Intensity, High Frequency)
