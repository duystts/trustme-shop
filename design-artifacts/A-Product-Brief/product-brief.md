# Product Brief - trustme-shop

**Phase 1: Project Foundation**
**Date:** 2026-03-15

---

## 1. Vision & Core Concept
**What is it?**
A fully functional online clothing e-commerce system, originally built as an academic project but structured like a production-ready application.

**The "Why" (Core Value):**
To provide a complete online shopping experience (browsing, cart, checkout, tracking) combined with a robust management system. The standout feature is an **AI Chat Widget** that offers tailored product recommendations, setting the project apart.

## 2. Target Audience & Business Customers
**Primary Users (Client Web):**
Retail customers who want to seamlessly browse, search, and purchase clothing items while receiving intelligent product suggestions via AI.

**Internal Users (Admin Web):**
Shop owners and staff members who need an efficient dashboard to manage products, categories, users, orders, and payments.

## 3. Positioning & Competitors
**Market Positioning:**
A clean, lightweight, and modern e-commerce platform inspired by local Vietnamese brands and international fashion brands (featuring light/clean aesthetics, white cards, and soft shadows).

## 4. Business Model & Success Metrics
**Business Model:**
Standard B2C retail e-commerce functionality (currently tailored for demonstration and grading purposes).

**Success Criteria:**
- Delivery of a complete end-to-end shopping flow (Client) and management flow (Admin).
- Successful integration of an AI product recommendation widget.
- Codebase quality (Java 21, Spring Boot 4.0.3, clear layered architecture) suitable for academic evaluation.

## 5. Constraints & Platform Requirements
**Technical Environment:**
- **Backend:** Java 21, Spring Boot 4.0.3, Maven.
- **Frontend Architecture:** SPA/SSR communicating via REST APIs.
- **Security:** Spring Security & JWT for Auth/Authorization.
- **Integrations:** Mock payments for now (no real gateways like VNPAY/Stripe required); basic email integration for password reset.
- **AI Constraints:** Widget on client web; can be simulated if real AI API is not feasible within the project timeframe.

## 6. Tone & Brand Personality
**Tone of Voice:**
Professional, helpful, and modern.
**Visual Direction:**
Clean, minimalistic, with ample whitespace and clear typography emphasizing product photography. AI Chat should feel unintrusive but readily available.
